About the Platform

Transparency, Accountability, Civic Engagement

Our Mission

The Lesotho Parliamentary Feedback and Transparency Platform is a secure online portal enabling citizens to send feedback, petitions, and proposals directly to Parliament Members. It enhances transparency, bolsters accountability, and stimulates civic engagement.

Secure Submissions

End-to-end encrypted channels for submitting and tracking petitions, comments, and proposals.

Public Participation

Open suggestion board where citizens can vote and contribute ideas for parliamentary improvement.

Real-Time Notifications

Instant updates on your feedback and petitions so you stay informed every step of the way.
